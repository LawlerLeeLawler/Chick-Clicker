What is Chick Clicker?

It's a fun clicker game with chickens that I'm developing.

Hatch chickens, collect eggs, and grow your farm in this incremental clicker idle game. Level up your flock, improve skills, and watch your farm thrive while you relax.

If you wish to support the development of Chick Clicker, please wishlist it on Steam.

Features

  • 🐔 Raise Chickens
  • Feed Chickens
  • Buy Upgrades
  • Automate then Relax
